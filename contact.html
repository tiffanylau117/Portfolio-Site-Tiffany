About

My Journey

I am a product designer & front end developer based in San Francisco, Bay Area.

I have fashion styling/design & customer service background. Having published in 4 indie magazines, I have expertise in translating abstract concepts into final designs, from paper mood boards to digital prints. Along with the experience working with clients in fashion, and as an in-house employee in customer service, it’s been a long journey improving people’s experiences. It was also my innate curiosity that drove me to transition to another form of design with new approaches & challenges. As a product designer, I'm interested in the importance of incorporating user- focused approaches, solution-based methods, and harmonic designs.

Feel free to connect and chat with me about design!
