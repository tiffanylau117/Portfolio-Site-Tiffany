My Journey

I am a product designer with front-end development skills based in San Francisco, Bay Area.

I have former fashion and customer service background. Having published in 4 indie magazines, I have expertise in translating abstract concepts into final designs, from paper mood boards to digital prints. Along with the experience working with clients in fashion, and as an in-house employee in customer service, it’s been a long journey improving people’s experiences. It was my innate curiosity that drove me to transition to another form of design with new approaches and challenges.

As a product designer, I believe in the importance of incorporating user-focused approaches, solution-based methods, and harmonic designs.

Besides designing, I'm a baker, video gamer, traveler and fitness enthusiast. Feel free to connect and chat with me about design or any of my hobbies!
